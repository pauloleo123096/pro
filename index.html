About LVLUP Pro

Founded by LeeYawn, LVLUP Pro is on a mission to make custom professional emails accessible to everyone—without the hassle of expensive domains or recurring monthly hosting fees.

How It Works:

1. Domain Procurement

We use your initial setup deposit to purchase and secure your desired domain name. This domain is registered entirely in your name. We will proactively inform you of the exact annual renewal fee, and if you ever decide you no longer need our services, we will gladly process a full domain transfer to you.

2. Email Routing Setup

Once your domain is secured, we handle the backend configuration, setting up your new custom email address to route directly to your existing inbox.

3. Seamless Integration

We provide comprehensive, easy-to-follow instructions on how to set up SMTP forwarding (via Google App Passwords) so you can seamlessly reply and "Send As" your new custom email directly from your existing inbox.

Important Note

The service we provide utilizes email SMTP forwarding using standard DNS setups. This means your new professional email acts as an alias connected to your existing inbox (like a standard Gmail account). If you prefer to have a completely separate, standalone inbox for your domain, that requires purchasing dedicated email hosting (which differs from email forwarding). Our standard service package focuses strictly on providing an affordable email forwarding service using your own domain.

FYI: Pricing Transparency & Renewals

We pride ourselves on having no recurring monthly subscriptions. Our service and setup fee is strictly one-time. However, if your selected package includes a custom domain (like .tech or .org), please note that internet domains require a standard annual renewal fee (typically $6.88 to $15 depending on the domain type) starting in Year 2. If you choose a package using our @pro.lvlupgames.org domain, you will never pay a renewal fee!

Terms & Conditions

1. Nature of Service: LVLUP Pro is a technical configuration service. We provide routing, not email hosting. Your mail will still live on your destination server (e.g., Gmail).

2. One-Time Fee & Renewals: Our setup service fee is a one-time payment. However, for tiers with custom domains, the fee covers Year 1 of the domain registration. Annual domain renewal fees thereafter are the strict responsibility of the customer. Domains that are not renewed will expire, and email forwarding will cease.

3. Reputation: You may not use aliases under the LVLUP Games network for spam, phishing, or illegal acts. Violation results in immediate termination.

4. Liability: We are not liable for outages from third-party providers like Google, Porkbun, or Cloudflare.

5. Privacy: We collect your name and email only for setup. We never see your Gmail password.

Privacy Policy & Data Handling ▾

Data Collection: We collect your full name, routing email, and optionally your phone number/messaging handle strictly for the purpose of fulfilling your order and coordinating your technical setup.

Third-Party Integrations: If your chosen package includes a custom domain registration, we will securely provide your name and email to our authorized registrar partner (Porkbun) to legally register the domain under your true identity. Account Security: LVLUP PRO will never ask for, require, or store the password to your personal destination inbox (e.g., your Google or Microsoft password). All SMTP integration is conducted via secure, application-specific passwords generated by you on your own device.

Data Retention: We adhere to standard data protection practices. Your contact details are kept strictly confidential and retained solely for ongoing service delivery, future technical support, and critical renewal notifications.
