Common Questions

What is Email Forwarding?

It's simple: emails sent to yourname@pro.lvlupgames.org are instantly redirected to your existing Gmail or Outlook inbox. You get the professional look without the monthly GSuite bill.

Can I reply using my professional alias?

Yes! Every LVLUP Pro plan includes a guide on how to configure "Send Mail As" in Gmail so you can actually send emails from your custom handle for free.

What does "One-time Setup" mean?

You pay us once to configure the complex DNS, SMTP, and routing infrastructure. We don't charge you a monthly management fee. (Note: For custom domains, you will still need to pay the annual registration fee to the domain registrar after the first year).
